New Scheme

PM Surya Ghar: Muft Bijli Yojana

The latest central government initiative aims to provide free electricity to households through rooftop solar systems.

Subsidy Structure
  • Up to 2kW: ₹30,000 per kW
  • Additional capacity (up to 3kW): ₹18,000 per kW
  • Total cap for >3kW: ₹78,000 (Maximum Subsidy)

How to Claim Your Subsidy

1
Registration

Register on the national portal with your electricity consumer number.

2
Application

Apply for rooftop solar installation and get feasibility approval.

3
Installation

Get the system installed by a registered vendor (like us!).

4
Inspection & Release

After net meter installation and inspection, subsidy is credited to your bank account.
