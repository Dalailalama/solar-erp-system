Everything You Need to Know Before Installing Solar

Aditi Sharma
March 10, 2024
5 min read

Switching to solar energy is one of the best investments homeowners can make today. Not only does it reduce your electricity bills, but it also increases your property value and contributes to a greener planet.

1. Understand Your Energy Consumption

Before installing solar panels, analyze your electricity bill. Look at your monthly usage in kilowatt-hours (kWh). This will help determine the size of the system you need.

Tip: Most average homes require a 3kW to 5kW system to offset 80-90% of their bill.

2. Assess Your Roof Condition

Solar panels are designed to last 25 years or more. Ensure your roof is in good condition to support the panels for this duration. Ideally, your roof should face south to capture maximum sunlight.

3. Know the Costs and Incentives

The cost of solar has dropped significantly. Additionally, government incentives like the PM Surya Ghar Yojana can subsidize a significant portion of the cost. Check our subsidies page for more details.

4. Choose the Right Installer

Work with certified and experienced installers who offer warranties on workmanship. A good installer will handle all the permits and net metering applications for you.

"Solar energy is not just about saving money; it's about investing in a sustainable future for the next generation."
