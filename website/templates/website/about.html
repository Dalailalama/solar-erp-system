About Ultra Rays Solar

Powering the Future with Clean, Renewable Energy

Our Story

From humble beginnings to an industry leader in solar energy solutions.

Founded in 2010, Ultra Rays Solar began with a simple yet powerful vision: to make solar energy accessible and affordable for everyone. What started as a small team of passionate engineers has grown into a leading solar installation company serving thousands of homes and businesses across the state.

Over the past decade, we have stayed true to our core belief that sustainable energy is not just a trend, but a necessity for a cleaner, healthier planet. We take pride in our precision engineering, customer-centric approach, and unwavering commitment to quality.

Our Mission

To accelerate the world's transition to sustainable energy by providing high-quality, efficient, and affordable solar solutions with exceptional customer service.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and innovative solar energy partner, empowering communities to achieve energy independence and a carbon-neutral future.
